Aggressive offers an inclusive fitness program that incorporates training principles from numerous training methods. A Strength and Conditioning program that uses CrossFit as a resource.

Book Class.

CONDADO LAGOON

Located in the heart of Condado, the lagoon has a wide path perfect for runners and bikers alike. Along the way you can stop at some of the fitness stations to get in a few squats along the way. The water stays nice and calm as it's sheltered from the waves so it's also a great place to swim for longer distances.

WOW SURF SCHOOL

Whether you are a first time or you want to advance your skills, WOW offers surf lessons and board rentals. They also offer jetski tours and jetski rentals.

Book a surfing lesson.
