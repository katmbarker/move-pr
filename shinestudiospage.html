SWIM BIKE WALK RUN

CONDADO LAGOON

Located in the heart of Condado, the lagoon has a wide path perfect for runners and bikers alike. Along the way you can stop at some of the fitness stations to get in a few squats along the way. The water stays nice and calm as it's sheltered from the waves so it's also a great place to swim for longer distances.

EL PASEO

El Paseo connects Condado to Old San Juan. With a brand new sidewalk and bike lane it's the perfect place for runners and bikers alike. The views over the ocean are incredible.

NATATORIUM

The San Juan Natatorium is an Olympic-level aquatic sports facility located in San Juan, Puerto Rico. Located in San Juan's Parque Central, it includes an Olympic-size 50-meter pool convertible to two, 25-meter pools, 10 lanes each 5 meterwide with permanent starting blocks.

PINONES PATH

It’s not surprising that biking in Piñones is a favorite activity of locals and tourists alike. This beautiful area, located just minutes east of Isla Verde, has beaches, mangroves, restaurants, and an 11-kilometer nature trail. A perfect place to ride your bike, walk or run.

EL LINEAL

The Bayamón River Lineal Reclamation follows along the historic Bayamon River. It consists of a linear walkway composed of two tracks, one for pedestrians and one for bicycles. It starts at highway PR 177 and ends at highway PR 22, occupying an approximate length of 9.0 kilometers.

WATER SPORTS

SPECTRUM SURF

Spectrum Surf School is a recreational surf school in San Juan, Puerto Rico. They offer surf lessons, guided surf tours, SUP lessons and rentals.

Book a surfing lesson.

WOW SURF SCHOOL

Whether you are a first time or you want to advance your skills, WOW offers surf lessons and board rentals. They also offer jetski tours and jetski rentals.

Book a surfing lesson.

PINE GROVE SURF CLUB

Pine Grove surf club offers everything from surfing lessona and rentals to snorkeling to paddleboarding tours and rentals and even Paddleboard Yoga!

Book a surfing lesson.

15 KNOTS KITEBOARDING

Located right on the beach in Ocean Park, 15 Knots is Puerto Rico's Endorsed Kiteboarding School specializing in Beginner Kiteboarding courses and lessons by certified instructors in San Juan.

Book a kiteboarding lesson.

SOTAVENTO

Sotavento offers both kiteboarding and windsurfing lessons. They also offer kayak, windsuring, kitboarding, and snorkel rentals.

Book a lesson.

OUTDOOR YOGA

BALA YOGA STUDIO

Bala Yoga offers outdoor yoga on the 1st Sunday of every month at 9:30am in Parque Del Indio in Condado. They also offer a more intense HIIT interval training prior to the yoga at 9:00am.

THE DREAMCATCHER

The Dreamcatcher hotel offers outdoor yoga classes in the mornings and occassionally classes under the stars. See if you can join one of their classes by contacting the hotel.

ACRO YOGA

Acro Yoga classes are offered every Thursday evening at 5:00pm at Parque La Ventana al Mar next to the Condado Vanderbilt Hotel. Get ready to get airborne if you try this one!

BEACH YOGA

Every Friday and Sunday evenings at 5:00pm join in a on a donation-based yoga class right in Ocean Park! Located by the Volleyball courts at the end of Calle Taft.
